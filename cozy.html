Jönköping
The two sisters Alicia and Sofia green grew up together on a small farm nearby Uppsala Sweden and has childhood been interested in making things by hand. It could be everything from clay vases to their own clothes and more. but the things they liked to do the most have always been to bake pastries and make their own candles. The two sisters already have a well-known bakery that they have owned since 2010 with the most wonderful smell of newly baked sweets. With the inspiration of the newly baked cinnamon rolls, they began to make candles with the same smell and started to sell them in the bakery at the darker end of 2020. the sales went wonderful and the customers were amazed by the smell. The candle family has since then grown and they now also have the scent of “Hallongråtta” and “Chokladboll” and has the thoughts of continuing on the Swedish Fika theme and ad more smells to the candle family. The candles have been a popular addition to the bakery so the sisters decided to start a company with it, so that everyone can take a part of the scent. since 2022 anyone can get a hand on a candle by going to the sister's website cozy.com. The candles with the scent of newly baked always make the home cozy, or is it cozy that makes the home? :)
